Question JSON builder

Fill in the fields and this tool builds a valid question/v1 record — a single-answer practice question, the most common type. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is sent anywhere.

Golden rule

Make every distractor a plausible "right-but-not-best" action, and make sure your correct answer is not the longest or most detailed option — length telegraphs the key.
